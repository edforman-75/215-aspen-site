How to Request Samples

On each Room & Board product page, click the fabric swatch you're interested in and look for "Order free swatches" or the swatch icon. You can order up to 10 free fabric samples per order. They ship via USPS within a few days.

For the Chilton coffee table, you'll choose your specific walnut slab online or in a showroom — each one is unique.
