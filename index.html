Palette

Color Direction

Warm walnut wood, oatmeal and warm gray upholstery, cognac and camel leather accents, matte black metal details. Walls: Benjamin Moore White Dove (sample on wall before committing).

Five Zones

Room Layout

The room functions as a single large rectangle with five activity zones organized around the freestanding stone fireplace — the visual heart of the space.

Zone 1

Living / Seating

Center of room, facing fireplace and TV. Sofa, two accent chairs, coffee table on area rug. Reconfigures for 16-person hybrid Zoom meetings.

Zone 2

Primary Dining + Workstation

Left of island facing cabinets. Extension table seats 4 daily, 8–10 for parties. Also serves as primary workspace with Baldy views.

Zone 3

Piano

East corner, unchanged. 1918 Steinway Model O with morning/midday light from two window walls. Floor lamp for sheet music.

Zone 4

Reading / Workstation 2

Near top of stairs. Adams Drop-Leaf Table does triple duty: desk, reading area, overflow dining for 4–6 guests at big gatherings.

Zone 5

Kitchen Counter

Curved concrete island with 3 new counter stools. Casual breakfast, party perching, meeting overflow.
